For the girl who wasn't just a girl

By Vibin Dave D

Published on: 2021-06-05

There was a girl Who felt to be a pearl Daring and dashing Like a glittering obsidian I used to be proud To be in her friend's list I used to sing her praise And it felt like a pure melody The one never said "I'm just a girl" But one day  Something slithered her heart But I didn't knew She'll turn into a moron Crying and weeping Forgetting who she was She feeded on her fears I waited and waited To see the rebirth of phoenix Now I feel the fear Of losing my old dear Remember this Life is a journey  You can't skip the darkness Figure out the short cut To the next junction of happiness! I'm waiting To see the girl who wasn't just a girl
