GIRL "FRIEND"

By Vibin Dave D

Published on: 2021-06-06

Had a friend.  A girl "friend". Not my lover.. But love was never lower...  our eyes saw friendship. Others assumed Titanic ship..   Society fixed a blunder ...  friendship was on gender ... she is your friend?!!! question was a viral trend.. But I wander why they wonder...    She was not my soulmate... A friend for fate.. Proof was necessary... Call her sista! For crooked mouths, it was a fiesta.. Hugging was a treason . shoulder for her tears was joke...   Society!! But our dear ones? Who should change? © Vibin Dave
