Moment

By Vibin Dave D

Published on: 2021-10-28

Those few seconds That loudness in silence Piercing through nerves Turning rational realm numb   Was I injured? Or was in a shock?  I only remember the fear Felt like a breeze slithering my throat Unable to utter a single word.   Maybe I was afraid of time Wished that moment will freeze.  Each moment gathered a million questions Each moment adding a billion adjectives   First time I felt the weakness Rational realm seemed brittle  All the theories I know Turned into fairy tales   All this time I thought People are searching for answers Now I knew People are searching for questions Which doesn't have answers   I woke up finally Summoning my consciousness "Are you even listening to me? " "No... I was thinking about something" "Always do this to me!!! "   She never knew...  That I was searching for more questions But I'll never understand how She's the answer to all those questions
