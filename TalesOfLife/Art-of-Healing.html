Art of Healing

By Vibin Dave D

Published on: 2021-06-06

Wound is born to heal. Life is live to feel. Once I saw you as mine. Now I am learning to say "I'm fine."   It was not long ago  And it was not just ego Whenever I think about loosing that hand. My body gives me a tremor with rhythm of a band.   Always heard love is mad. But i learnt living without it, is bad. I was broken when u found me. You patched it saying "we".   Many had a question...why you? But i was sure that it was only you. I owe you alot of respect. Because you made me see life in a new aspect.   Most comfortable turned to be mother's womb. But I felt the same inside your hands. It came all the way..... From kissing to missing. From fighting to crying.   It is not a scratch to heal. And It takes alot to feel. I don't know whether you know. Hope is my medicine to heal.   I am not broken now... i don't know how.. I learnt to live. Waiting for love © Vibin Dave
